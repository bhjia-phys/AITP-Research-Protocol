- ✓ `REQ-L2RET-01`: AITP supports real graph traversal and relation-aware
  expansion over the seeded `L2` memory surface instead of stopping at flat hit
  lookup.

  raw storage.

  and seeded graph state without silently bypassing trust-boundary rules.
- ✓ `REQ-VERIFY-01`: The milestone closes with a non-mocked verification path
  proving the matured graph retrieval and consultation surface through
  production code.
