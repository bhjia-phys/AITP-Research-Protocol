# Research Question Contract

## Purpose

Define the active research question in a way that prevents silent scope drift.

## Minimum fields

- `question_id`
- `title`
- `topic_slug`
- `status`
- `question`
- `scope`
- `assumptions`
- `non_goals`
- `target_layers`

## Why it matters

If the question is not explicit, the agent can silently mutate the task.
